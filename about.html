Hi I'm Rachele Contini

Artist based in DC

I was born in Washington DC, but I grew up in a trilingual household and traveled a lot growing up. Growing up with 3 different cultures has influenced my artwork by making me more open to different mediums. Most of my works are based off of my own experiences. I do portraits of people I've met, paintings of places I've seen, and multi-media piecies for abstract pieces. While my naturalistic paintings have a loose quality to them, my abstract pieces have a significance to every detail. By doing that, I try to find balance between different styles of art. I am curious about almost everything, and love to learn. Besides creating art, I teach language and I'm currently learning to code. A few of my hobbies include discovering new music/artists, watching movies, and yoga.
