About me

I received my degree in Studio Art from the University of Maryland in 2021. As an artist, my goal is to be versatile. I don't just stick to paintings, but I like to draw, experiment with mixed media or even digital media. A lot of my works are based off of my own experiences, portraits of people I've met, or paintings of places I've seen. While my naturalistic paintings have a loose quality to them, my abstract pieces have a significance to every detail. By doing that, I try to find balance between different styles of art.
