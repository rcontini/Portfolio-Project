About me

I received my degree in Studio Art from the University of Maryland in 2021. As an artist, my goal is to be versatile. Art is a tool for me to remember the places, people, events in my life. For that reason, I don't stick to only one medium, instead I like to draw, paint, experiment with mixed media and even digital media in order to capture the object of my piece perfectly.
